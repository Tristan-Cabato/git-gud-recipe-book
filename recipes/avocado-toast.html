Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Sea salt flakes
  • Black pepper
  • Optional: red pepper flakes
  • Optional: feta cheese crumbles

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove pit, and scoop into a bowl.
  3. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper until creamy but still chunky.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on each toast slice.
  5. Top with halved cherry tomatoes.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt flakes.
  7. Add red pepper flakes or feta cheese if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy! üåü
