Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until garlic is golden (about 2 minutes).
  4. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  5. Add drained pasta to the skillet with garlic oil.
  6. Toss with parsley and a splash of pasta water to create a silky sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, serve with grated cheese.
